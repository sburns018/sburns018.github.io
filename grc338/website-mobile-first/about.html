What's BurnsBucha?

BurnsBucha is local small business located in the heart of San Luis Obispo County. Both being young students the Burns' wanted to share their love and recipie of their craft kombucha for the world to taste.

Kombucha History

Kombucha has been made and consumed by humans since 220 B.C. It was first made in northeast China where people drank it for its health benefits, which include detoxification and increased energy.

For the past 2,000 years and more people have been sharing their recipes and methods for creating kombucha, and spreading the word about how it may help you feel your best. This is our BurnsBucha goal!

What does "Burns" mean in BurnsBucha?

Emily and Shelby Burns are the creators of BurnsBucha, known as the "Burns Girls". Their goal was to make their kombucha more accesible to everyone as well as give knowledge and nutrition to make a healthy lifestyle.
